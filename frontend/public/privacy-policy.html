Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 5, 2025

LaunchAd ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LaunchAd platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use LaunchAd, we may collect:

  • Facebook Account Information: When you connect via Facebook OAuth, we receive your Facebook user ID, name, and access to your Facebook Pages that you authorize.
  • Landing Page URLs: URLs you submit for analysis to generate ad campaigns.
  • Campaign Data: Ad settings you configure including budget, target locations, and duration.
  • Generated Content: AI-generated ad creatives (text and images) created for your campaigns.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Analyze your landing page content to generate relevant ad creatives
  • Create and publish ad campaigns to Meta Ads Manager on your behalf
  • Store generated images temporarily for campaign creation
  • Improve our service and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our platform:

  • Meta/Facebook: For authentication (OAuth) and publishing ads via the Marketing API
  • Google Cloud AI: For content analysis and image generation (Gemini, Imagen)
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): For storing generated ad images (S3)

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Facebook session data is stored temporarily in memory during your active session
  • Generated images are stored on AWS S3
  • We do not permanently store your Facebook access tokens on our servers
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide our service.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Facebook Data

When you disconnect your Facebook account or revoke access, we delete your Facebook session data. You can also manage app permissions directly in your Facebook settings.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session (e.g., Facebook authentication). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
