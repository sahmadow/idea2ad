│  [Secondary line - italic]         │  ← Below, 60% size of above
│                                    │

│                                    │

Top text: 5-8 words max, bold sans-serif, high contrast (white on dark bar or dark on light bar)
Secondary line: "And you can, too." — italic or lighter weight, 60% size of headline
CTA strip: Bottom band with action text ("Start Your AI Podcast Now") + product logo
Font size ratio: Headline 100% → Secondary 60% → CTA 50%
Safe zone: Keep text out of middle 60% so face/subject isn't obscured
Contrast: Use semi-transparent bar behind text if image is busy
